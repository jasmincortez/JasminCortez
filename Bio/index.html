Mini Bio

Jasmin is a Brazilian-German actor, VO artist and singer-songwriter.
In the supporting cast for feature film Fox Sisters (2023) and prime video's 5x Comédia (2021), Jasmin entered the international theatre scene in the main cast of the musical Monstersongs! (2022) and the play Noir Suspicions (2022).
She had her theatre debut in Pentesiléia (2020) after graduating from CAL drama school in Brazil.
At 13, Jasmin started studying musical theatre and followed that starring in Frozen-Uma Aventura Musical (2018).
Jasmin's single Volta e Meia is featured in prime video's Making My Life's Script.
